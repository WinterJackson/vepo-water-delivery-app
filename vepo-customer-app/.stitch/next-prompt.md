The main home dashboard for the Vepo customer app.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
When generating screens, ALWAYS enforce:
- A strictly modern, minimal, high-contrast aesthetic.
- **DARK MODE**: Use Deep Charcoal/Black backgrounds (#121212) with slightly lighter dark surfaces (#1e1e1e) for cards. Text should be stark white or very light gray.
- The use of the brand blue (#3498db) for the primary Call to Action or active elements.
- The use of yellow/gold (#f1c40f) for ratings or premium highlights.
- **Typography:** Refined and scaled down for mobile. Do NOT use overly massive bold text; keep headlines at a crisp, elegant 20-22px size. 
- **Padding/Margins:** Ensure flawless, generous padding inside cards (especially the Featured Offer Banner) so text never touches the edges and feels highly professional.
- Circular icons and avatars.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** User's delivery address at the top with a subtle dropdown arrow, and a circular profile avatar on the right.
2. **Search Bar:** A pill-shaped search bar floating below the header.
3. **Favourites / Quick Access:** A horizontal scrolling section featuring the user's "Favourite" refill stations or wholesalers. These should be distinct (perhaps circular vendor avatars with a small heart badge or a "Favourites" section title).
4. **Quick Categories (Bento Grid):** A horizontally scrolling or compact grid of visually rich category cards (e.g., 20L Refill, Dispenser Bottles, Accessories).
5. **Featured Offer Banner:** A premium-looking promotional banner utilizing the brand blue or gold accent. Ensure the text inside this banner has EXCELLENT padding and margin.
6. **Nearby Vendors (Vertical List):** A list of nearby water vendors. Each card shows the vendor image, name, rating (gold star), delivery time (e.g., "15-20 min"), and a subtle "Order" button.
7. **Bottom Navigation Bar:** Minimalist tab bar with Home (active in brand blue), Search, Cart, Orders, and Account.
